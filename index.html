Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shared Web Hosting ?

With Shared Hosting, a single server hosts multiple websites, which means you share the server resources with other websites.

Can I host multiple Websites within single Shared Hosting plan ?

Yes, you can host multiple websites on our Platinum & Advance Plan by adding secondary domains through your hosting control panel.

Can I transfer my existing Website to HostiGate India ?

You can migrate your Web Hosting package from another Web Hosting provider to HostiGate India. For further details, please contact our Support team.

Does a Control Panel include a Website Builder ?

Yes, you can use SitePad Website Builder, which is a drag & drop editor to publish static pages to your hosting account through FTP. SitePad can be enabled through the ‘Softaculous Apps Installer’.

What Domains are available from HostiGate ?

At HostiGate, you can register brand new domain names using hundreds of popular TLDs. In our Marketplace, you will find thousands of domain names that have already been registered but are now for sale. While .com domains are available, of course, you'll also see options using .net, .us, and many more.

Which Domain extension is best for Personal Websites

The .com extension is also one of the most credible domain extensions and it instantly makes your website appear more trustworthy. And if you consider for a moment that you will share your new website with everyone you know, it makes sense to use .com extensions to make it easier for people to remember.

How long does it to take to transfer a Domain ?

A domain transfer usually takes about 5-7 days to complete. There are a few variables involved — including the cooperation of the prior registrar, and the domain owner ensuring the domain is unlocked with the correct authorization code provided.
